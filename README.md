<h1>Flowodoro</h1>
<p>Spin to the pomodoro technique with taking into account flow state</p>

<h3>How it works</h3>
You set your work/break coefficient and when you feel tired - only then you stop your session. The amound of break is calculated through formula

```
Work session * Coefficient = Small Break
```

For example:

```
47 minutes of work * 0.3 = 14 minutes of break
```

Big break is also configurable

<h3>How to use</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Set your session requirements</li>
  <ul>
    <li>Set minimum work session duration</li>
    <li>Set your coefficient of work/break</li>
    <li>Set how many work sessions before big break</li>
  </ul>
  <li>Start</li>
  <li>When work time has passed you can proceed to break session</li>
</ul>
